INFORMATION

The Confederation Africaine de Football (“CAF”) has delayed the opening of the first phase of the ticket sales for the TotalEnergies CAF Africa Cup of Nations Morocco 2025 to finalize important details that will ensure the best possible experience for fans.

CAF is working with the Local Organizing Committee (“LOC”) and various stakeholders and will announce a new date for the first phase of the ticket sales in due course.

CAF apologizes for any inconvenience caused.
